Democratic Republic of Congo

The Democratic Republic of Congo (DR Congo) is a landlocked country located in the heart of Africa, renowned for its rich biodiversity and vast tropical rainforests. It is one of the few places in the world where wild bonobos, our closest great ape relatives, can still be found thriving in their natural habitat. These remarkable primates, known for their peaceful and social behavior, inhabit the dense forests of the Congo Basin, making DR Congo a critical region for wildlife conservation and ecological research.
